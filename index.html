⛳ Computer Vision & Biomechanics

Golf Swing Analyzer

An intelligent computer vision pipeline powered by XGBoost, MediaPipe, and Bidirectional LSTMs to evaluate golf swings with professional side-by-side synchronization.

📦 View ML Models Hub 📊 Pro & Benchmark Dataset

⚡ Key Architecture Capabilities

🤖

Binary Gatekeeper

XGBoost classifier (99.0% accuracy) running 2.0-second rolling window validation score aggregation to reject non-golf videos and clips exceeding 60 seconds.

🧠

BiLSTM Milestone Locator

Bidirectional LSTM neural network operating on 108 kinematic coordinate features to pinpoint all 8 key swing events with an overall MAE of 2.66 frames.

📐

Biomechanical Scorecard

Calculates Spine Tilt loss, Lead Arm straightness, Hip Sway displacement, Bobbing Head stability, and Foot Weight Transfer at Finish.
